FAQ

Is there a limit to how much I can listen?

There is no limit to how much you can listen. Enjoy your music without restrictions.

Do I need an account?

You can explore some parts of the app without an account, but creating one unlocks the full experience. With an account, you can save your favorite songs, create playlists, receive personalized recommendations, and interact with other users.

How do smart playlists work?

Smart playlists are automatically generated based on your listening habits and preferences. They update in real-time to include new songs that match your taste.

Can I create my own playlists?

Yes, you can create and manage your own playlists once you have an account.

Can I share music with friends?

Yes, you can share music with your friends.
